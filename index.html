What is Form 540NR?

California Form 540NR is the income tax return used by nonresidents and part-year residents of California who have California-source income. This is the 2025 tax year version of the form, to be filed in 2026.

Who Should Use This Form?

  • Nonresidents of California with income from California sources
  • Part-year residents who moved into or out of California during 2025
  • Military personnel domiciled outside California but stationed in California

Filing Deadline: The deadline to file your 2025 California tax return is typically April 15, 2026. Extensions may be available by filing Form FTB 3519.

Related Forms

You may also need Schedule CA (540NR), Schedule S, and the Form 540NR Instructions.
